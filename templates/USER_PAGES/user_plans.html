Frequently Asked Questions
Can I cancel my plan at any time? ⌄
Yes! You can cancel or downgrade your plan at any time. Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period, and you won't be charged again.
What happens when I exceed my query limit? ⌄
On the Free plan, further queries will be paused until the next billing cycle. You can upgrade at any time to instantly restore access and get more capacity.
Do my unused queries roll over? ⌄
No, query limits reset at the beginning of each monthly billing cycle. Unused queries do not roll over to the next month.
What payment methods are accepted? ⌄
We accept UPI, net banking, all major credit/debit cards (VISA, Mastercard, RuPay), and institutional purchase orders for Enterprise plans.

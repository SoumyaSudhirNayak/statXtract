* The screen is divided into TWO sections:

1. LEFT PANEL (Fixed Sidebar)

   * Always visible (fixed)
   * Contains navigation menu
   * Must be RESIZABLE (user can drag to increase/decrease width)

2. RIGHT PANEL (Dynamic Content Area)

   * Occupies remaining space
   * Content changes based on selected menu item
   * Scrollable independently

Design a modern web UI theme for a project named "SPATXTRACT" that blends a professional government feel with subtle dynamic visuals and a soft modern aesthetic.

---

## BACKGROUND DESIGN (KEY FEATURE)

* Base background should always feel WHITE or near-white.

* Use very light pastel tones layered subtly:

  * soft blue tint
  * soft green tint
  * soft yellow tint
  * soft peach/red tint

* Background should NOT look colorful at first glance.

* It should appear white, but gently shift colors over time.

---

## DYNAMIC BACKGROUND BEHAVIOR

* Add slow, smooth color transitions across the background:

  * Transition duration: 8–15 seconds
  * Use gradient blending between pastel tones

* Add a subtle animated gradient layer:

  * Very low opacity
  * Soft movement (floating / flowing effect)

* Mouse interaction:

  * When user moves cursor, a soft glow trail appears behind cursor
  * Glow should be diffused, blurred, and pastel-colored
  * No sharp or bright colors

* Overall effect:

  * Calm, smooth, almost invisible motion
  * Feels modern but not distracting

---

## CARD / BOX DESIGN (IMPORTANT)

* Cards should have:

  * Soft borders
  * Slight elevation (shadow)

* Add a VERY subtle golden glow effect:

  * Only visible slightly
  * Should not look flashy or shiny

---

## HOVER EFFECTS

On hover over cards/buttons:

* Slight upward lift (translateY)
* Slight increase in shadow
* Soft glow intensifies slightly (golden tone)
* Smooth transition (0.2–0.3s)

Avoid:

* Bounce animations
* Strong scaling
* Harsh glowing

---

## BUTTON STYLE

* Clean rectangular or slightly rounded buttons
* On hover:

  * Slight brightness increase
  * Subtle glow

---

## BACKGROUND INTERACTION (ADVANCED)

* When mouse moves:

  * Create soft radial gradient following cursor
  * Colors:

    * pastel blue
    * pastel green
    * pastel yellow
  * Very low opacity
  * Blend smoothly into background

* No sharp edges or strong contrast

---

## OVERALL VISUAL FEEL

* 80% clean government UI
* 20% modern dynamic UI
* Calm, premium, soft
* No neon colors
* No heavy gradients
* No flashy animations

---

## TYPOGRAPHY (VERY IMPORTANT)

HEADER FONT:

* Use a strong, modern, slightly bold font
* Recommended:

  * "Poppins" (semi-bold or bold)
    OR
  * "Inter" (bold)

Usage:

* Titles
* Navbar (SPATXTRACT logo text)

---

BODY FONT:

* Clean, highly readable font
* Recommended:

  * "Inter" (regular)
    OR
  * "Roboto"

Usage:

* Forms
* Tables
* Content text

---

ACCENT / SECONDARY TEXT:

* Slightly lighter weight of same font
* Maintain consistency

---

## TEXT STYLE RULES

* Keep text sharp and readable
* Avoid decorative fonts
* Maintain hierarchy:

  * Large bold headers
  * Medium subheadings
  * Small body text

---

## ANIMATION RULES

* Keep all animations subtle
* Duration: 0.2–0.5s for interactions
* Background animation: slow (8–15s)

Avoid:

* Fast movement
* Flashy transitions
* Overuse of effects

---

## END GOAL

The UI should feel:

* Professional and trustworthy (government-level)
* Calm and clean
* Slightly futuristic and interactive
* Elegant with soft glowing and moving background
* Comfortable for long usage without distraction
